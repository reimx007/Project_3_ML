Conclusion

Overall, the single Linear Regression model produced the closest results for predicted sales values but only for some states. Considering only previous home sales, this model would work. However, there are multiple factors in the value of a home, like economic stability, job market, housing inventory, and location. Housing prices are complex and have multiple potential impacts. With this being our first attempt at applying a machine learning model we recognize the limitations inherent in our project's predictions.
